Axis Bank Credit Card Eligibility


The following are the eligibility criteria to qualify for the Axis Bank Credit Card:


  • Cards are issued to both resident and non-resident Indians.
  • The age of the applicant should be between 18 years to 70 years.
  • The add-on cardholder should be over 15/18 years, depending on the card type.
  • The minimum annual income required depends on the card type.
  • Some of the cards like Axis Bank Reserve Card are issued on invitation only. If the customer is eligible for these cards, the banner will be displayed on their FreeCharge mobile app.
  • Some cards are issued against Fixed Deposits. The value of the Fixed Deposits should be between Rs. 20,000 to Rs. 25,00,000. The card will not be issued against tax saver Fixed Deposits, Flexi Deposits, and against Fixed Deposits in the name of HUFs/ Society/ Trusts/ Companies.
  • Credit Score should be above 750.


Axis Bank Credit Card Eligibility


The following are the Documents Required for the Axis Bank Credit Card:


  • Colour photographs of the applicant
  • PAN card photocopy/ Form 60
  • Latest payslip/ Form 16 /ITR copy as income proof
  • Any one of the following address proof:
  • Ration card/Passport/Electricity bill/Telephone bill (landline)
  • PAN card/Driving license/Passport/Aadhaar Card
