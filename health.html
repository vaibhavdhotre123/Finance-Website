What is Health Health Insurance

  • Health Health Insurance is a product by Health Insurance Companies to cover the medical and surgical expenses
    incurred by the insurer. Health is the most unpredictable aspect of one's life. Anybody
    can encounter a serious health issue at any stage of life. Health problems need not crop up only in old age.
  • If not any health issue, it could be hospitalisation due to an accident. Meeting medical expenses which appear out-of-the-blue is not in the reach of everyone. At least not at present times, when the cost of medical treatment is soaring high day by day.
  • The product that covers the expenses that arise out of such emergencies is Health Health Insurance. Periodical payment of premium as stipulated by the Health Insurance Company has to be made. The payment of the premium could be either monthly, quarterly, half-yearly or yearly.
  • The Health Insurance company either pays the medical expenses incurred directly to the hospital or will reimburse the expenses to the insured individual. Flexible coverage for ailments/disease is provided. Some Health Health Insurance companies offer a cover for as many as 80 and above surgical procedures and as many as 30 and above critical illness.
  • Health Health Insurance can be a saviour when such critical emergencies emerge at the eleventh hour. If you have a huge family responsibility with regard to every member of the family, then going in for Health Health Insurance for the entire family is recommended.

Features and ADvantages of Health Health Insurance Policy

  • With a Health Health Insurance Policy, you will be able to get medical treatment in the best of hospitals without being worried about the cost of the treatment.

  • Any medical emergencies can be handled with great ease even at times when you are in a disastrous financial situation.

  • Imagine you come across a situation when you are diagnosed with a health issue which can be treated in specialised hospitals whose cost of medical treatment seems unaffordable. This is at such times that Health Health Insurance comes handy.

  • The cashless facility of the Health Health Insurance policy is the best feature. All the expenses incurred in the course of your medical treatment will be directly remitted to the hospital and you will not have to worry about paying the hospital first and then claiming reimbursement from the Health Insurance Company.



    The main features of a Health Health Insurance policy are:

  • Cover for expenses incurred prior to hospitalisation and post hospitalisation The expenses incurred 30 to 60 days prior to hospitalisation and post hospitalisation will be reimbursed upon submission of relevant bills and receipts.

    • Treatment without payment - Cashless treatment:
    Under this feature, treatment will be provided in the hospitals listed in the Health Insurance policy without the payment of cash. This relieves you of the burden of arranging for cash to meet the expenses. The hospital authorities will send a pre-authorisation request with the details of treatment and the cost. Pre-authorisation from the company will be as good as paying cash for the treatment. The Health Insurance company will remit the treatment expenses directly to the hospital.
